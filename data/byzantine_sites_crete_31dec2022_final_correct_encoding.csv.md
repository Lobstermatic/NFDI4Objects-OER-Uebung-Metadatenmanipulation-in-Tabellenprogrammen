# Metadaten des Datensatzes "byzantine_sites_crete_31dec2022_encoding.csv"
Quelle: Klontza-Jaklova, Vera, und Adam Geisler. „SYNECDEMUS NOVUS“. .xls-file. Pandora, 12. Januar 2023. https://www.doi.org/10.48493/b8mn-4n64
Veränderungen: Änderung des Character Encoding auf UTF-8; Speicherung als .csv-file.
Verändert durch: Henrike Backhaus am 2025-10-24.
Attribute und Werte (auf Grundlage der Datenpublikation):
- ID: a unique numerical identifier of each entry
- Site: site name as mentioned in the newest publication
- Latitude, Longitude: geographic coordinates in WGS84 Decimal Degrees
- Exact location: Y – within 100m deviation, N – more than 100m deviation.
- Literary source: Y – mentioned by ancient author(s)
- Site type: Settlement, City, Hamlet, Fortification, Monastery, Church, Cemetery, Cistern, Water Construction, Coin
- Chronology from: Minimum possible date in the frame of the Byzantine Period (A.D. 324-1204). When the site was used before A.D. 324, the number 200 was filled in.
- Chronology to: Maximal possible date in the frame of the Byzantine Period (A.D. 324 – 1204). When the site was used after the Byzantine Period, the number 1205.
- Bibliography - title nr. 1: The newest publication mentioning the site 
- Bibliography – title nr. 2: The first publication mentioning the site
- DOI – if it was assigned
- Notes – some detailed remarks
